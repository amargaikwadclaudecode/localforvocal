How It Works

1

You Post

Describe what you need, set your radius and urgency.

2

Shops Get Notified

All matching shops within your radius receive your requirement instantly.

3

They Respond

Interested shops reply with availability, pricing and delivery options.

4

You Choose

Compare responses and pick the best shop for your needs.

💡 Tips for Better Responses

  • Be specific — mention brand, size, quantity
  • Add a photo if possible
  • Set a realistic budget range
  • Choose the right urgency
  • Keep your phone reachable
